About Us

This exam revision website began to be developed in May 2024. It was founded by a year 10 student interested in cyber security (specifically ethical hacking) and programming (particularly in python, although also in JavaScript). It was made for the primary purpose of gaining knowledge of web development, CSS, and HTML. However it served the secondary purpose of being a useful tool for GCSE Revision.
